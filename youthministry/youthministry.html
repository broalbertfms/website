Our Mission

A Space Where
Every Young Person
Belongs

The Marist Youth Ministry (MYM) is a vibrant expression of the Marist charism, dedicated to accompanying young people on their journey of faith and human development.

Inspired by St. Marcellin Champagnat and following the way of Mary, we strive to create spaces where youth feel welcomed, valued, and empowered to become "good Christians and virtuous citizens."

Journeying with the Young
Faith Formation
Accompanying young people in their personal relationship with God.
Human Development
Empowering youth to discover their gifts and contribute to society.
Community Building
Creating a family spirit where every voice is heard and valued.
Core Pillars

How We Accompany

Presence

To be among the young as one who loves and respects them — being fully available in their joys and struggles, being "another Christ" for them in every moment.

Simplicity

Our way of living and working is uncomplicated, honest, and humble — reflecting the Marist spirit that values sincerity over complexity, and love over ambition.

Family Spirit

Creating a community where everyone feels they belong — characterized by love, forgiveness, and mutual respect. No young person is ever alone in a Marist community.

"Touch the hearts of young people and you change the world."

— Inspired by St. Marcellin Champagnat

Ministry Activities

Each program is designed to nurture faith, build community, and empower young people to discover the Marist spirit in their daily lives.

🙏
Spirituality
Youth Retreats & Recollections
Quiet, transformative experiences that help young people encounter God in prayer, silence, and community.
🌍

🎉

The Marist Way

Walking with
Mary's Spirit

The Marist Youth Ministry is rooted in the spirit of Mary — humble, nurturing, and deeply present to the young. Like Mary at Cana, we anticipate the needs of youth and act quietly but powerfully in their lives.

Our programs span retreats, service immersions, leadership formation, arts ministry, and ecological advocacy — all carried out with the Marist hallmarks of presence, simplicity, and family spirit.

Faith-Centered

All programs flow from a deep encounter with the living God and the example of Mary.

Regionally Connected

Young people from across East Asia united by a common mission and shared charism.

Holistic Growth

Nurturing the whole person — spiritual, intellectual, emotional, and social development.

Service-Driven

Putting love into action through outreach, solidarity, and care for the marginalized.

About Us

The Marist Brothers of East Asia serve through education and pastoral ministry, continuing the legacy of St. Marcellin Champagnat, our Founder.
