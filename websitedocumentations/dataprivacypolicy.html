Data Privacy and Cookie Policy

"Protecting your information with transparency and respect."

Effective Date: February 2026

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

We respect your right to privacy and protect personal data in accordance with Republic Act No. 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012), National Privacy Commission regulations, CHED policies, and Catholic principles of dignity and stewardship.

2. Personal Data We Collect

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Contact Number
  • Institutional Affiliation

3. Purpose of Collection

Personal data is collected solely for legitimate educational, pastoral, administrative, and institutional purposes. We do not use personal data for commercial marketing.

4. Lawful Basis for Processing

Consent

Direct user permission via forms or interactions.

Legal Obligations

Requirements under local and international law.

Cookies and Tracking

A. Facebook (Meta) Cookies

Data via Facebook Pixel is stored on Meta's secure servers.

B. Email Data

Stored on hosting servers and secure Email Service Providers.

C. Website Cookies

Stored locally for functionality and analytics (e.g. Google Analytics).

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or trade personal data. Information is shared only with authorized personnel, trusted service providers under confidentiality, or when required by law.

6. Data Subject Rights

You have the right to be informed, access, correct, object, request deletion, and file a complaint with the National Privacy Commission.

7. Donor Privacy and Profiles

When you donate to Marist East Asia, we collect information to process your contribution and maintain a history of your support. This includes:

  • Contact information and donation history.
  • Donor Profiles: We may maintain a profile of your giving history to provide donation receipts, tax documentation, and personalized updates on our missions.
  • Financial Security: We do not store credit card numbers on our servers. All financial transactions are handled by secure, PCI-compliant third-party payment processors.

Your activities as a donor are also governed by our Terms and Conditions.
