Japan

"Building Bridges Through Education and Faith"

Our Presence

A Presence Rooted in Service

The Marist Brothers have served in Japan for decades, contributing to Catholic education and interfaith dialogue in a predominantly non-Christian society. Their witness of lived charity and humble service has opened many doors for evangelization and human promotion.

Through schools and community programs, the Brothers build relationships grounded in respect, integrity, and the Marist spirit of making Jesus known and loved.

Apostolate

Core Ministries

Catholic Schools

Providing quality Marist education that nurtures the whole person in the Japanese academic tradition.

Youth Ministry

Engaging Japanese youth in values formation, retreats, and community service activities.

Dialogue

Promoting interfaith and intercultural dialogue in the Japanese context, fostering mutual understanding.
