OUR DNA

The Pillars of Marist Life

Rooted in the charism of St. Marcellin, these pillars define how we serve and live in community.

Presence

Being close to the young, understanding their needs, and walking alongside them in their journey.

Simplicity

Living modestly and authentically, avoiding ostentation while maintaining dignity and commitment.

Family Spirit

Creating communities of care, mutual support, and belonging where everyone feels valued.

Common Inquiries

Frequently Asked Questions

We are a religious institute of men dedicated to the mission of making Jesus Christ known and loved through the education of youth, particularly those most neglected. The East Asia Province includes various countries in the region.

You can join us as a religious brother, a lay volunteer, or support us through donations and prayer. Visit our "Vocations" or "Join Us" pages to learn more about the different pathways.

We have communities and schools in the Philippines, South Korea, Japan, Malaysia, Singapore, and China. Each region focuses on meeting the specific needs of local youth.

100% of your donation goes directly towards our educational scholarships, outreach programs for marginalized children, and sustaining our mission centers across the province.
