The Ransang Initiative

Building Futures in Barangay Ransang.

The Marist Palawan - Malutok Mission is a long-standing initiative by the Marist Brothers in the Philippines, specifically focused on supporting the Pala'wan indigenous community in Sitio Malutok, Barangay Ransang, in the municipality of Rizal.

Our presence here is not just about building schools—it is about empowering a community to defend its heritage, educate its youth, and steward the Last Frontier of the Philippines for generations to come.

The Urgent Need

Why the Last Frontier Needs You?

The Pala'wan community is at a critical crossroads. Industrial encroachment threatens their ancestral lands, while economic hardship forces many youths to abandon their education and heritage.

By stepping in, you aren't just giving a donation—you are giving a community the tools to defend their home. You are ensuring that an indigenous child can read, write, and eventually lead their people into a sustainable future without losing their cultural identity.

How You Can Help

  • Fund a Scholar

    A monthly or one-time donation provides essential school supplies, tuition, and food allowances for Pala'wan students.

  • Raise Awareness

    Share our mission. The more people know about the struggles and triumphs of Sitio Malutok, the stronger their voice becomes.

  • Volunteer

    Join our medical missions, educational drives, or administrative efforts to directly impact the community.

Transparency and Impact

Where Your Donation Goes

60%

Indigenous Scholarships

Direct funding for primary, secondary, and tertiary education for the Pala'wan youth, breaking the cycle of poverty.

25%

Ancestral Domain

Legal and community advocacy to prevent industrial exploitation and preserve the sacred forests of Sitio Malutok.

15%

Cultural Heritage

Supporting local artisans, language preservation, and traditional community gatherings that keep the Pala'wan identity alive.
