Regional Presence

Our Communities Across East Asia

Living the Marist Charism Together

The Marist Brothers of East Asia live and minister in diverse communities across the region. Each community is a living witness to the Gospel, animated by the spirit of St. Marcellin Champagnat and the values of prayer, simplicity, and family spirit.

Our communities are rooted in local cultures while remaining connected to the global Marist family, sharing resources, charism, and mission across borders.

Community Life Values

Prayer

Daily communal prayer is the foundation of every Marist community, anchoring our life in God's presence.

Fraternity

We live as brothers, supporting one another and creating homes of welcome and belonging for all.

Mission

Every community is sent on mission — to serve the young, to educate, and to proclaim the Gospel by our lives.
