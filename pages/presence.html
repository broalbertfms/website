Our Presence in East Asia

Serving Communities Across the Region

Marist Presence Across East Asia

The Marist Brothers have established a strong presence in East Asia, with schools, parishes, and pastoral centers serving thousands of young people and their families.

Our Communities and Institutions

Hong Kong

Established: 1960s

Marist College Hong Kong is one of our flagship institutions, serving over 2,000 students from Primary to Secondary levels.

  • Marist College Hong Kong
  • Pastoral centers
  • Parish outreach programs

China

Established: 1980s

Growing Marist presence in Shanghai and other major cities, adapting our charism to the Chinese context.

  • Marist School Shanghai
  • Educational partnerships
  • Community development programs

Singapore

Established: 1970s

Marist Academy Singapore serves as a hub for our East Asia region, hosting training and formation programs.

  • Marist Academy Singapore
  • Regional office
  • International coordination center

Taiwan

Established: 1980s

Marist School Taipei provides quality Catholic education and spiritual formation to young Taiwanese.

  • Marist School Taipei
  • Youth pastoral programs
  • Community engagement

Our Ministries

Education

Four schools providing quality Catholic education and character formation to young people across East Asia.

Pastoral Care

Parish ministry, youth formation, and spiritual guidance serving communities across the region.

Social Service

Community outreach programs addressing poverty, education gaps, and social welfare needs.

Regional Initiatives

Inter-School Cooperation

Our schools collaborate on academic exchanges, cultural programs, and shared formation activities, fostering a strong Marist network across East Asia.

International Partnerships

Partnerships with Marist communities worldwide, sharing best practices and strengthening our global charism.

Teacher Formation

Continuous professional development and spiritual formation for educators ensuring quality and faith-based teaching.

Lay Partnership

Growing involvement of lay partners in our mission, expanding our capacity to serve and deepening our community impact.

About Us

The Marist Brothers of East Asia serve through education and pastoral ministry, continuing the legacy of St. Marcellin Champagnat, our Founder.
