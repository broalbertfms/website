Formation Team

Meet the dedicated Marist Brothers who walk alongside our candidates, providing guidance and spiritual nourishment throughout their journey.

Guides on the Journey

The Formation Team of the Marist Brothers of East Asia is a dedicated group of experienced Brothers who accompany candidates at every stage of their journey — from initial discernment through aspirancy, novitiate, scholasticate, and beyond.

Each member of the team brings a wealth of experience in Marist education, spirituality, and community life. They are companions, mentors, and brothers who walk alongside candidates with patience, wisdom, and the spirit of Champagnat.
